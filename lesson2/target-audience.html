Description of Target Audience

The target audience for this project is the general public who live in the South Eastern part of Idaho. Whether you are an avid outdoorsman, planning an outing, or simply trying to plan your day, you will find this page useful.

5 Scenarios

  • Scenario 1:

    • A Biology student is planning a trip to the mountains to observe various local animals. He is looking for a weather forecast for the next few days.

  • Scenario 2:

    • A parent is checking the weather in order to pick out their child's clothes for the day. They need to know if rain is expected and what the high and low temperatures are forcasted to be.

  • Scenario 3:

    • A family is planning a surprise birthday party for their young twin children. In order to plan either an outdoor or indoor party, they need to know the weather forcasted for the next few days.

  • Scenario 4:

    • There is a soccer game scheduled for this weekend and the weather is going to be unpredictable. The coach is checking on the weather forecast.

  • Scenario 5:

    • The local church group is planning a yard sale and needs to know if they should set up inside or outside. They need to know the weather forecast for the next few days.
